3.Imotski Blue Lake,Croatia 

	When soluble rocks (limestone for example) dissolve, a lake may be formed.

	Imotski is a few kilometres from the Croatian coast and the beautiful town of 
	Makarska which is worth a visit! This natural pool is 800 metres long and 500 metres wide.

4. Porto Moniz,Portugal 
 
	You will love Madeira Islands for the perfect climate throughout the year. 
	It is neither too hot nor too cold. You will also love Madeira Islands for
	its exceptional hotels such as the Reid's Palace where Winston Churchill 
	used to stay. You will enjoy the long beaches located in Porto Santo, go
	shopping in Funchal or trekking in the mountains of Madeira.

	Madeira Islands also have amazing natural pools: Porto Moniz is one of them.
	The sea rushes into the pools made of volcanic lava and forms magnificent 
	swimming pools accessible to all even with reduced mobility.
