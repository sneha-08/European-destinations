Best destinations for food lovers in europe 

	
		For lovers of wine, good food, for those who wish to leave the beaten track and discover new sensations, 
	new perfumes, new tastes; for those who like to be surprised and discover the best of Europe, for those who are looking
		for unique and authentic experiences, we have selected the best travel destinations for food lovers in Europe.
	
		Join in the harvest in Bordeaux, wine tasting in the Douro Valley, looking for truffles in the woods of Croatia, try
	the local cuisine but also culinary innovations in Porto, Florence, Paris, Bergen, Athens and Dubrovnik. You will love the wines 
	of Alsace when visiting Colmar and Strasbourg but also delicious pancakes and Normandy cider in the Mont Saint-Michel. 
	Savour a soft ice cream in Milan, Rome or Florence with a delicious Prosseco or enjoy a Brussels waffle with traditional beer.
